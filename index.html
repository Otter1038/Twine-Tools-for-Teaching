A collection of free, browser-based Twine games built for writing classrooms. Report for duty and get to writing.

The Tools

★ Tool No. 1 MLA
📚
Citation Station

A step-by-step MLA citation generator covering journal articles, books, websites, YouTube, social media, and newspaper sources. Build your Works Cited page one citation at a time.

★ Tool No. 2 ENC

Peer Review

A guided peer review tool that walks students through giving structured, meaningful feedback on their classmates' writing — moving beyond "it was good" into real critical response.
